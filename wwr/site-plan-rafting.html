Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer an online space where customers can purchase and schedule our whitewater rafting trips.

Audience

Middle class or higher groups, individuals, and families with children aged 5 and up. For a family or group, what they want is to spend time with their loved ones with a side of adrenaline. Any potential customer who comes onto the site expects to find a page that allows them to pay for and schedule a rafting trip easily. They'll want to see costs, location, and they (especially parents) want to know if it's safe. Each category of customers I mentioned consist of people who want thrilling experiences with others to escape the mundane, but in a controlled environment with professionals. They should be able to at least access the site on their desktop or laptop and hopefully on a mobile device.
